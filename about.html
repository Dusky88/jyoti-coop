What We Do

We empower women in Nepal by providing business loans, organizing skills training with partner organizations, leading community development initiatives, and offering healthcare support through our member-funded assistance pool.

Supporting Local Women Businesses

We are dedicated to empowering women entrepreneurs across Nepal by providing financial support and business promotion services. Our cooperative offers accessible loans to women-owned businesses, helping them grow and thrive in their communities.

  • Microfinance loans for women entrepreneurs
  • Support for bamboo utensil businesses
  • Funding for crochet and handicraft enterprises
  • Financial assistance for bead and ornament makers
  • Business promotion and market linkage support
  • Capital for establishing and expanding local stalls

Skills Training & Capacity Building

Through strategic partnerships with governmental organizations and civil society, we organize comprehensive training programs that equip women with practical skills for sustainable livelihoods and economic independence.

  • Collaboration with The Women's Foundation Nepal
  • Partnership with local NGOs for skill development
  • Coordination with Municipality departments
  • Vocational training in various crafts and trades
  • Entrepreneurship and business management workshops
  • Livelihood enhancement programs

Community Development & Social Welfare

We actively engage our women members in creating positive change through community initiatives, health programs, and awareness campaigns that benefit entire neighborhoods and promote social well-being.

  • Communal cleaning and sanitation campaigns
  • Women's rights and empowerment awareness programs
  • Free health camps in collaboration with health organizations
  • Blood testing and health screening services
  • Polio vaccination and immunization drives
  • Partnership with NGOs for community welfare projects

Healthcare Support & Financial Assistance

Understanding the financial burden that chronic health conditions can place on families, we have established a member-funded assistance program that provides crucial support during medical emergencies and long-term health challenges.

  • Member-supported helping fund pool
  • Financial aid for chronic health diseases
  • Emergency medical assistance for members
  • Reducing healthcare-related financial burden
  • Compassionate support during health crises
  • Community-driven healthcare solidarity

Our Mission

To empower women through economic opportunities, skill development, and collaborative support, creating sustainable livelihoods and stronger communities.

Our Vision

A society where every woman has the opportunity to thrive economically, socially, and personally, contributing to a more equitable and prosperous Nepal.
